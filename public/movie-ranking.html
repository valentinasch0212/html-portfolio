The Best Movies According To Valentina

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Interestellar

If you're into sci-fi, this movie is for you. The photography and plotwist are mind blowing and it is a really good story about time travelling and the love of your family.

La la land

The most exciting and heartbreaking musical that I've ever seen. The acting of Emma Stone and Ryan Gosling made the movie a piece of art.

Mean Girls

An iconic comedy of all-time. It is a 'must' if you want to be more into pop culture.
