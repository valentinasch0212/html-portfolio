The Best Movies According to Valentina

My top 5 movies of all time.

Inglorious basterds

A Tarantino classic. I recommend this film if you are into action movies and topics like the second world war.

Interstellar

If you like movies that give you a headache because of the amount of information that you receive, this is for you. Interstellar is one of the best films made by Cristopher Nolan and has a really interesting plot about time travelling and science.

La la land

One of the best romance films that i've ever seen. With stars like Emma Stone and Ryan Gosling, this movie has the perfect combination of good acting, an interesting plot and soundtrack.

The arrival

It is a really good film if you are a sci-fi lover. Its plot includes topics like time travelling, science, Aliens and political conflicts.

Gone girl

This film directed by David Fincher is the perfect suspense story that you are looking for. The most relevant aspect of this movie is its plot-twist.
